NearbyFYI

An API for our cities and towns

We're like Google for cities and towns

Sort of. NearbyFYI is an API that provides access to structured data from documents generated by our local city and town government. If you've needed access to structured information and data about your city or town government, then you know how painful it can be.

How do we help you?

You won't spend time:

  • Creating screen scrapers
  • Setting up and maintaining web crawlers
  • Building document processing pipelines
  • Extracting text from HTML, PDF, Excel and Word documents
  • Denormalizing data sources
  • Performing named entity extraction with a local government model
  • Classifying documents using city and town terms
  • Archiving documents you find
  • Deploying and maintaining a search engine
  • Supporting an API

We're focused on providing access to the highest quality, structured data about the thousands of cities and towns in the United States and the millions of documents they generate.

How is this different?

In contrast to current city open data solutions, our platform is free for cities, towns and their constituents. We provide low cost, software as a service solutions for municipalities that replace existing expensive tools. These tools make the collection of data easier and we then charge a fee to commercial users of the API. We believe that this new model flips what was a previous expense line item into an overall cost savings for cities and towns.
